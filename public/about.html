About Dashmon

Dashmon is a lightweight monitoring dashboard designed to help teams keep internet‑facing services and infrastructure online. It focuses on clarity: what’s up, what’s down, what’s slow, and what changed.

What we monitor

  • Websites and HTTP endpoints
  • Servers and network devices (as configured)
  • Uptime history and performance trends
  • Alerts to email and integrations
